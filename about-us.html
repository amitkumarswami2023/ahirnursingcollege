About Us

Ahir College of Nursing ABOUT US

Welcome to Ahir College of Nursing, Rewari, Haryana.

The Ahir College Society known for its unhindered service in the field of education and sports with impeccable record and integrity through genarations is going to establish a new institute named ‘Ahir College of Nursing’. The Ahir College Society has vast experience of managing other institutions. Among these, Ahir College, Rewari carries the legacy of providing education and opportunities to students from diverse backgrounds especially South Haryana which was considered to be a backward area at one time and Ahir College with a rich history was the sole college of this area. The college is initially starting two programmes, B.Sc. Nursing and General Nursing and Midwifery (GNM) with approval of the competent authorities. Our college has a lush green campus with serene environment and is well equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure, latest laboratories and library facilities as well as sports facilities. Our well qualified, experienced and compassionate faculty members are committed to provide high-quality education and training to aspiring healthcare professionals equipping them with the knowledge, skills, compassion and ethics required to excel in their careers. With a strong focus on academic excellence, clinical practice and community service, we aim to produce competent and caring healthcare professionals who will make a positive impact in the healthcare sector.

Our Mission

An institution dedicated to shape the future of healthcare professionals.

  • To provide a supportive and inclusive learning environment that fosters academic excellence and personal growth.
  • To create committed, compassionate & skillful healthcare providers with ethics who make a difference in building a healthy community and nation.
  • To promote research, innovation, and best practices in nursing education and healthcare.
